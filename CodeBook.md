* Activity - a character string naming the activity the participant engaged in

  * tBodyGyroJerk-std()-X,tBodyGyroJerk-std()-Y,tBodyGyroJerk-std()-Z,
  * tBodyAccMag-mean(),
  * tBodyAccMag-std(),
  * tGravityAccMag-mean(),
  * tGravityAccMag-std(),

  * tBodyAccJerkMag-std(),
